ALDERMAN.AI — FAQ DOWNLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
========================================

You've downloaded our FAQ document — a markdown (.md) file
designed to be uploaded to an AI platform like ChatGPT or
Claude for a fully interactive deep-dive into the company.

This isn't a normal document. When you upload it to an AI,
the file tells the AI how to behave: it will ask your consent
to act as a representative of alderman.ai, or — if you prefer —
to just answer your questions neutrally. Both modes give you
the same information from the same source.


NEW TO AI? HERE'S HOW
---------------------

1. Open chatgpt.com / claude.ai / or Google Gemini. Free accounts work fine.

2. Start a new chat.

3. Click the paperclip icon (or "+" menu) to upload the
   alderman-ai-faq.md file you just downloaded.

4. Ask anything — see sample prompts below.


SAMPLE PROMPTS TO START WITH
----------------------------

Copy and paste any of these to get going:

  "Tell me about Alderman AI"
  "How do the lessons work?"
  "What does it cost?"
  "Who are these lessons for?"
  "Why ai as a language, not as software?"
  "What does a typical session look like?"
  "How is this different from an online course?"
  "Tell me about Alex"
